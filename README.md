What Is God Box?
God Box is a sacred journaling and prayer practice system — available in both physical journal and digital workbook formats — designed for people who have learned to make even their faith presentable.
This is the space where that stops.
No performance. No cleanup. Just you, your words, and God.

  The Manifesto
“You don’t have to be eloquent to be heard. You just have to be honest.”
God Box is where you stop curating your faith. You journal beautifully. You pray eloquently. You’ve learned to make even your brokenness presentable.
This is the space where none of that is required.
Write the ugly prayer. Release the thing you’ve been holding. Track the moments He showed up anyway.
Your avatar reflects you. Your words are yours. This is between you and God.
